About Me

Hey, I’m Yusuf Yehia — I design interfaces and build the code behind them. Based in Cairo, but my work doesn’t believe in borders.

For the past 16 months, I’ve been wrestling messy ideas into clean flows and turning “can we even do this?” into “oh, that actually works.” My thing is making digital stuff look good and feel effortless.

Outside the screen? I’m usually digging into design subcultures, arguing with new tools, or wandering Cairo with headphones on and a camera in hand.
